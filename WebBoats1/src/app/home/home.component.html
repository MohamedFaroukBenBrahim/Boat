A Global Crisis Plastic Pollution

Our oceans are overwhelmed by trillions of pieces of plastic, and the situation is rapidly worsening. This pollution wreaks havoc on marine wildlife and ecosystems, with plastics breaking down into microplastics that persist for decades. These tiny particles are often ingested by marine life, ultimately making their way into the human food chain. Tackling this urgent issue is essential to protect our planet and its future..
